I am a student at Brigham Young University Idaho studying Computer Science with and emphasis in machine learning. I plan on working in education technology after I graduate. My Daedalus Library project is a step towards that goal. I am trying to combine my love of education and technology to help students learn. It isn't obvious to me that we have found the best way to intergrade technology into our study habits and I hope to offer a better solution. Other projects steam from a similar curousity about how to mix technologies in a way that encourages simple living in by natural intuition.
